About

Vision

TO Women in Tech is a grassroots movement that acts on principle to combat discrimination in the workplace with little regard for optics. It does this by shifting thought paradigms to normalize intersectionally gender-equal workplace cultures from the ground up in technology and STEM fields. We would ultimately like this to further advance merit-based employment and career advancement approaches in the workplace with no undertones of bias.

Mission

TO Women in Tech motivates our youth to be receptive to change by inspiring them, further engaging disengaged individuals in technology and STEM fields. To raise the standard of learning and social awareness, it provides connectivity and affordability, and in turn accessibility to educational resources and career advancement opportunities. It uses an approach that ricochets to educate our youth by raising awareness towards biases that push back on intersectional gender-equality in the workplace. We would like to grow from intersectional gender-equality to cover the larger umbrella of equity, diversity, inclusion, tackling issues at the root.
